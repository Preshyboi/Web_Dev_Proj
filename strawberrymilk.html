Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 2 cups milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • 2 tablespoons honey or sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  • Additional strawberries for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the fresh strawberries, milk, honey or sugar, and vanilla extract (if using).
  2. Blend until smooth and well combined.
  3. If desired, add ice cubes to the blender and blend until the mixture is chilled.
  4. Pour the strawberry milk into glasses.
  5. Garnish with additional sliced strawberries, if desired.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
